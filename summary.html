Summary

Summary:

The Smartphone patent war as a whole is a pretty difficult one t analyze. The number of patents are increasing day by day. The total number of patents at present is fairly a large one. Therefore the number of patent wars is also increasing day by day. They are growing in a very fast pace. It is good to have patents to protect our work but the number of patents should be limited.

The following video is a debate on smartphone patents

It is unethical to use patented work but it is even unethical to get patents for everything even if it doesn't deserve to get patent. So there should be a proper way to grant the patents. There should be some more conditions to be applied to grant a patent. The patent term should be decreased and the authority of the Patent and Trademark Office to try patent cases should be expanded.

The patent eligibility condition has to be refined. The patent should be granted keeping in mind the necessity of having patents to protect it.

There is also a necessity to bring a provision similar to fourth fair use guideline to patent so that the patent wars can be decreased and the technology can be extended making new innovations.
