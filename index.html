Smart Phone Patents

Shravya Mahankali

Abstract:

This online presentation site discusses about Smartphone patents. The U.S. Patent System was initially created to secure a designer or inventor's original thought and the piece of the pie that idea may generate in an industry, in this manner additionally advancing innovative technological ideas.

The Smartphone patents licensing and litigation is an ongoing war between various smartphone manufacturers including Apple, Motorola, Google, Samsung, Sony, Microsoft, Nokia, HTC, LG and among others in patent infringement. In a patent dispute over technology used in android mobile phones between Motorola and Apple, Richard Posner, a judge on the Chicago-based 7th U.S. Circuit Court of Appeals, said "An injunction that imposes greater costs on the defendant than it confers benefits on the plaintiff reduces net social welfare."

This paper will examine profound on whether it is valid point to consider in patent cases. This online presentation also discusses on whether a provision similar to fourth fair use guideline apply to patents. An analysis will be presented on changes that are to be made to smartphone patents for more development in the technology.
